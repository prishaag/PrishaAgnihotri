My Projects

StoryGen

My friends and I built a Node.js/React Native interactive learning app for children during the MHacks Hackathon. The app generates stories based on the input of the child using the app- if they want a story about dinosaurs and rainbows, they will have it! We've used the ChatGPT API, NLP, as well as some text to speech packages to help children listen, read, and learn syntax for their generated story. Building this app was so fun- it was a great first app to build!

Submarine

During my freshman year, I participated in ENGR 100 Robotics Mechanisms, a course I now TA! In this class, my team built a submarine prototype with temperature and pressure sensors to address water pollution, a crucial global issue. We designed a ballast system using a syringe and motor for depth control, collecting precise data through oversampling. Our project was super cool, and I really enjoyed working on it.
